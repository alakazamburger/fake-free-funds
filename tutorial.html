The Basics

Hi, welcome back.

There are a few things you should know about this website.


"Fake Free Funds"?

Ignore the unnecessarily alliterative name for now. Fake Free Funds, or FFF, is the main currency used on the website.
Every hour you're allowed to press the "Mine" button, and after an hour you will have earned 1 FFF.
You must manually come back to collect your FFF, and to restart the timer.


Automation Limit (AL)

Once you've earned enough FFF, you can spend it on increasing your automation limit.
This limit increases how much FFF is automatically mined, but you must watch a 30-second advert to start the automation.

For example, when you first start, your AL is 1. This means every hour you have to check back on the website to claim your FFF.
But once you spend 10 FFF on upgrading your AL, you only have to check back once every 2 hours.
You can still check back at any amount of hours you wish, but to restart the automation you will have to watch another advert.
The amount of automated collections will be visible in the top left (eventually).


AL Pricing Formula

The price of your next AL is as follows (where n is your current AL):
cost of next upgrade = floor(10(n^1.1))
For example, if n = 3:
cost of next upgrade = floor(10(n^1.1)) = floor(10(3^1.1)) = floor(10(3.3484)) = floor(33.484) = 33
Therefore the cost of the next upgrade is 33.
